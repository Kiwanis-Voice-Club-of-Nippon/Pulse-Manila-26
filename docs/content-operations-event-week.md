# Content Operations Guide

## Goal

Keep Pulse fast, accurate, and calm during event week.

## Operating rule

Pulse should surface information faster, not reinterpret official information.

## Daily rhythm

### Before doors open

- confirm the day schedule against the official program
- confirm venue and room labels
- pin only the announcements that materially affect floor movement

### During the day

- post operational changes immediately
- keep announcement copy short
- update ASPAC highlights if a priority session changes
- unpin outdated items quickly

### End of day

- archive expired notices
- confirm the next day schedule
- stage morning announcements in draft form

## Announcement quality bar

Good announcement:

- changes attendee behavior
- can be understood in under 10 seconds
- includes timing and venue context

Bad announcement:

- repeats general marketing copy
- duplicates static brochure text
- creates ambiguity against official notices

## Escalation rule

If there is any conflict between Pulse content and official communication, the official communication wins and Pulse should be updated or pulled back immediately.
